Gnocchi Genovese

Gnocchi Pesto alla Genovese with chicken

This magnificient Italian dish combines potato gnocchi with diced chicken breast, a zesty Genovese pesto, and topped with some mozzarella cheese.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 skinless boneless chicken breast, diced
  • salt and ground pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons chicken broth
  • 1 jar pesto
  • 1 package potato gnocchi
  • 1 package mozarella

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a pan, meanwhile season chicken with salt and pepper
  2. Fry chicken until no longer raw, 7 to 10 minutes. remove chicken and put to side when cooked
  3. Pour chicken broth into the pan, bring to a boil and cook until reduced by half.
  4. Return chicken into pan, and stir pesto within the mixture. remove from heat when mixed
  5. Bring water to a boil and cook gnocchi until floating, rougly 3 minutes
  6. Strain water and put gnocchi into a bowl, with the pesto on top. Serve with Mozzarella.

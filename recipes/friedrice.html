Chicken fried rice

Amazing fried rice with chicken breast and assorted vegetables

Are you looking for another healthy dinner option for you and your family? This stir fry is a fan favorite in my household. Easily customizable if you would like, substitute the brown rice for any other style of rice, choose and mix assorted vegetables, and even substitute the chicken for any other protein.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups brown rice
  • 2 skinless chicken breasts
  • Assorted vegetables (mushroom, pepper, carrot, broccoli, cauliflower, corn, zucchini, or others)
  • Garlic and Onion
  • Oyster sauce and Soya sauce
  • Olive Oil
  • 1 teaspoon sugar if desired for sweetness

Instructions

  1. Cook 2 cups brown rice in rice cooker
  2. Dice and marinade chicken breast in oyster sauce
  3. Cook chicken breast over medium heat in frying pan until fully cooked but still juicy
  4. Heat oil in a wok over medium high heat and start frying veggies
  5. Add the cooked chicken breast, and cooked rice
  6. Add salt, pepper, oyster and soya sauce, (sugar if desired), and stir well, making sure every bit of rice is seasoned
  7. Serve and enjoy, garnish with green onions if desire
